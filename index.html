How to Play Tigers and Goats

Introduction

- Tigers and Goats is a strategic board game where one player controls 3-4 tigers and the other controls 15-20 goats.

Objective

- Tigers: Capture all goats by jumping over them.

- Goats: Block all tigers so they can't move.

Phases

- Placement Phase: Goats are placed on the board one by one, alternating with tiger moves.

- Movement Phase: Both players move their pieces to adjacent positions.

Rules

- Tigers can jump over goats to capture them (one per turn).

- Goats can only move after all are placed on the board.

- Game ends when all goats are captured or tigers are blocked.
